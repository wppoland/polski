Polish e-commerce legal compliance for WooCommerce — unit prices, Omnibus directive, legal checkboxes, withdrawal rights, tax display, and more.

== Description ==

Spolszczony makes your WooCommerce store compliant with Polish e-commerce law. It provides:

* **Omnibus Directive** — Automatically track and display the lowest price from the last 30 days when a product is on sale.
* **Unit Prices** — Display price per unit (cena jednostkowa) as required by Polish consumer law.
* **Legal Checkboxes** — Configurable consent checkboxes for checkout, registration, and reviews.
* **Checkout Compliance** — Correct order button text ("Zamawiam z obowiązkiem zapłaty") and terms acceptance.
* **14-Day Withdrawal** — Online withdrawal form and automated confirmation emails.
* **Tax Display** — Brutto/netto toggle, VAT notices, small business exemption (zwolnienie podmiotowe).
* **Delivery Times** — Per-product delivery time display.
* **Legal Pages** — Auto-generate Regulamin, Polityka prywatności, and more.
* **GPSR Compliance** — Manufacturer and safety information display.
* **Plugin Integrations** — Works with WC Price History, Omnibus by iworks, Flexible Checkout Fields, and more.

= PRO Features =

* PDF Invoices (Faktura VAT, Korygująca, Paragon)
* KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur) integration
* NIP validation via GUS/REGON API
* InPost, DPD, DHL, Poczta Polska shipping integrations
* Multi-step checkout
* Accounting integrations (wFirma, Fakturownia, iFirma)

== Installation ==

1. Upload `spolszczony` to `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to Spolszczony in the admin menu and follow the setup wizard
